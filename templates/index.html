உயிர் எழுத்துக்கள் 12

12 Vowels (Uyir Ezhuthukkal) representing the core sounds of Tamil. Including ஃ (Aayutha Ezhuthu), there are 13 letters in this section.

📌 ஃ — ஆயுத எழுத்து (Aayutha Ezhuthu) ஃ is the unique 13th letter. It is neither a vowel nor a consonant — it stands alone. Example words: அஃது (that), எஃகு (steel). Pronounced as a soft aspirated glottal stop. Unicode: U+0B83.

18 pure consonants — each carries a புள்ளி (dot) suppressing the inherent vowel.
